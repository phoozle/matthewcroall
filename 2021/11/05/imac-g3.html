iMac G3 Refurbishment - WIP

05 Nov 2021

The iMac G3 in my opinion is the greatest computers of the last 20 years with it’s functional yet sleek design and bold ambitions (lacking a Floppy Drive).

I never owned an iMac G3 growing up but my cousin had one.

I was PC boy at the time and I never wanted to admit it was better in anyway. I think retrospectively I realise how great a machine it was and how much I enjoyed using it; even though it couldn’t play my favourite games.

I picked up a Flower Power iMac G3 reluctantly not realising how rare and special it was in the G3 collectors community; it is second to the Dalmatian.
